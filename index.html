01. About Me

I am a freelance developer and media creator who builds things across the digital landscape. From web architectures and mobile apps to 2D game programming, graphic design, and video editing, I am hands-on with technology and media. My journey started in elementary school, teaching myself to navigate the web, play PC games, and learn game modding entirely on my own. By junior high, I was writing code, and by high school, I was building functional web and mobile applications.

My approach to software is deeply fueled by a lifelong love for pop culture, including games, music, and movies. I don't just build applications. I understand how algorithms engage users and how game loops dictate experience. While AI allows me to ship complex features in days rather than weeks, I believe a true developer's level lies in understanding core logic and system flow. This foundation lets me adapt, learn new tech stacks rapidly, and deliver high-quality work that stands out.

Beyond code and media, I have a formal background in teaching, holding a degree in Informatics Engineering Education. This unique blend of tech expertise and pedagogical training makes me just as comfortable explaining complex architectures or mentoring teams as I am programming systems.
